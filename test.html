How to be a sigma man...?

To be a sigma man, embrace independence, rely on yourself, and value solitude. Stay true to your values, avoid societal expectations, and build confidence through discipline and self-improvement. Control your emotions, understand others, and maintain strong personal boundaries. Focus on meaningful goals and work toward them with determination. Respect others but avoid seeking validation or engaging in unnecessary conflicts. A sigma man prioritizes self-growth and purpose above all else.
